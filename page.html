          Genesis10 is currently seeking a Junior Software Engineer with our client in the financial industry located in New York, NY. This is a 12 + month contract position.

          • Interpret Business Requirement Documents (BRDs)
          • Communicate with stakeholders to determine accuracy of testing requirements
          • Communicate with developers to keep them abreast of testing work and problems uncovered
          • Build out testing suites and then running test cases through the suites developed by themselves or by others as needed
          • Build out automated tools/processes to contribute the end-to-end testing process in collaboration with other teams
          • Investigate issues in our trading stack using SQL/Kafka/Java to diagnose the loci of the issues and subsequently communicating with teams needed to fix issues as they arise
          • Track of issues identified using JIRA and multiple databases (MYSQL/Oracle)

          Requirements:

          • A background in Software Development
          • Preferably from a major financial institution
          • BA in CS or Engineering
          • Preference given to Advanced Degrees
          • Experience coding in Java/Python/Groovy/OOPs
          • Advanced coding in Java/Python or any other OOP and Basic
          • Intermediate SQL experience

          Desired skills:

          • Knowledge of FIX protocol
          • CAT/OATS
          • Financial Knowledge
          • Experience presenting results in a group setting while adhering to industry best practices

          Pay Range: $49.86 - $57.86
